Tournamen Director Information

To become a USCF Tournament Director at the club level, you must meet these requirements:
  • Be a USCF Member
  • Obtain and Read the USCF Official Rules of Chess
  • Complete the Club TD Application form
  • Email the form to the USCF: tdcert@uschess.org
